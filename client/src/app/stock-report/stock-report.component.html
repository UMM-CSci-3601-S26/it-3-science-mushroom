Stock Report

Welcome to the Stock Reports page! Here, you can view the current stock levels of all items in the inventory, as well as save and export reports.

Below, you can choose to Generate or Generate & Download the current report by clicking the appropriate button in the "Generation Actions" section. Generating will create a PDF/XLSX of the report and upload it to the server, while Generating & Downloading will create a PDF/XLSX and save it to your computer.

Once you have generated a report, it will appear in the "Saved Reports" section, where you can then Save or Delete the report. You may also download or delete all reports at once by using the appropriate button in the "Downloading Actions" or "Deletion Actions" sections.

Please note! The Saved Reports list may take a moment to update after generating or deleting reports. If you don't see your report appear or disappear right away, please wait a few seconds and it should update. If it doesn't, refresh your page.
