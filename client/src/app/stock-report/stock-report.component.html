Stock Report

Welcome to the Stock Reports page! Here, you can view the current stock levels of all items in the inventory, as well as save and export reports.

"Finalize Report" will prompt you for information for the current report (such as the name) and save it to the server.

You may then download the report / past reports in CSV or PDF format by opening the "Saved Reports" dropdown and clicking the appropriate "Download" button next to the report you want.
